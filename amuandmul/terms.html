THOSE ELIGIBLE TO APPLY

An upstream investor who has acquired an oil prospecting license and must have completed a minimum amount of work on the concession.

WHO MAY APPLY FOR CRUDE OIL LICENSE

Local Refineries International Refineries Internationally recognized Oil & Gas traders​

WHO MAY PURCHASE NIGERIAN CRUDE OIL

A bonafide end user who owns a refinery and sales outlet. An established and
globally recognised large volume trader proof of its global network, its
operations and volumes of crude oil handled in the last three years. ​​
